CVPR 2013 Call for Workshop Proposals

Proposals for workshops are solicited for the 2013 IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR 2013) in Portland, Oregon. Workshops will take place on June 23-24 and June 28, 2013, at the same venue as the main CVPR conference.

Appropriate subjects for workshops include topics from computer vision as well as related fields or application areas. We solicit workshops that show a significant cross-section of the community and whose organizers have a strong track record in the field. Proposals that emphasize formats and/or programs that are unique to workshops, such as a large proportion of invited speakers or being based on a competition, are specifically encouraged. Such proposals will be favored over those that offer a traditional conference-type format.

Workshop proposals can be sent to the Workshops Chair, Ce Liu (cel..@microsoft.com.). A proposal should include the following information:
(1) Title, topic and sample call for papers;
(2) Justification of the workshop, including the relationship to the previous ones, the novelty of the proposed workshop, and the reason why the proposed workshop may interest the community.
(3) Names and contacts of the organizers;
(4) Tentative program committee, invited speakers if any;
(5) Estimated attendance;
(6) Estimated number of submissions and acceptance rate;
(7) Estimated breakdown among oral, poster presentations, and invited talks;
(8) Proposed length of the workshop, and date restrictions
(9) Special space and equipment request if any.

The deadline for all workshop proposal submission is October 19, 2012. Decisions will be made by December 2012. For any additional information, please contact the Workshops Chair, Ce Liu (cel...@microsoft.com).
