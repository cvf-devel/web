Sponsorship and Exhibitor Information

Sponsorship Information
We have several levels of corporate sponsorship that you may consider:

Gold Level ($8,000 & above)
  • 3 free registrations
  • Sponsorship of the Best Paper awards
  • free exhibit table!
  • Special recognition during banquet and other ceremonies
  • Logo on the CVPR website, logo on the handbook and conference proceedings
Silver Level ($4,000)
  • 2 free registrations
  • Sponsorship of the Best Reviewer awards
  • Special recognition during banquet and other ceremonies
  • Logo on the CVPR website, logo on the handbook and conference proceedings
Bronze Level ($2,000)
  • 1 free registration
  • Logo on the CVPR website, logo on the handbook and conference proceedings

We can direct your donation to student travel grants and other student activities as you prefer. We recently established a Computer Vision Fund to make the best use of donations. We can tailor sponsorship options based on your request as well.

CVPR bestows five awards (best paper, best paper honorable mention, best student paper, best student paper honorable mention, and Longuet-Higgins). We assign the awards on a first-in first-choice basis among the Gold sponsors and priority will be given to higher donation amounts. Sponsorship of the awards will be recognized during the conference and we will invite the sponsoring party to present the award.

Sponsor logos will appear as soon as the support is confirmed. Logos will be listed with respect to the donation amounts and sign-up dates.

If interested, please contact Fatih Porikli (fatih...@ieee.org) for sponsorship.



Exhibitor Information
CVPR has a strong tradition of hosting exhibits of advanced computer vision technology, products, and publications. Our exhibit tables are strategically-placed to foster interaction between the exhibitors and conference attendees, ensuring maximum exposure and interaction. We particularly encourage companies and other commercial entities or laboratories to participate in showcasing their products.

The main difference between the demos and exhibits categories is that demos are usually related to specific research papers (not necessarily presented at this CVPR), while exhibits are related to products.

Exhibitor tables are $1200. The exhibitors must agree to the terms and conditions in the Exhibitor Prospectus during the registration process. All registrations are made ONLINE (coming soon) and payment is by check, credit card or wire. The deadline for exhibit applications is May 10, 2013.

Exhibits must be staffed during the multiple Exhibit/Poster/Demo sessions each day:
    Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday - June 25, 26, 27, 2013
See the schedule for more details.

CVPR 2013 is offering discount Exhibit tables for Corporate Sponsors:
  • Gold sponsor = 1 free exhibit table
  • Silver = $500/ exhibit table
  • Bronze = $1000/ exhibit table
If interested, contact Ginger Boult (cvpr12 xatx vast.uccs.edu)

Shipping instructions will be emailed to all registered exhibitors in May 2013.

Questions? Email Ginger -- cvpr12 xatx vast.uccs.edu
