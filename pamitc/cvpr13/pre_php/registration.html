Author Registration
Conference papers - all conference papers *MUST* have at least one registered author (this can be a student) no later than the conference paper camera-ready deadline (April 12, 2013). One person can register multiple papers as long as that person is a co-author on each of the papers.
Workshop papers - all workshop papers *MUST* have at least one registered author (this can be a student) no later than the workshop camera-ready deadline (May 8, 2013).
Short courses - all short courses *MUST* have at least one registered organizer (this can be a student).

  1. Your FULL name including salutation (Mr., Ms., Mrs.)
  2. Your paper number
  3. Whether you want a hard copy, a faxed version or an pdf emailed to you
