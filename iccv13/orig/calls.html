Call for workshops.
Due date: May 3, 2013

Proposals for workshops are solicited for the 2013 IEEE International Conference on Computer Vision (ICCV 2013) in Sydney, Australia. Workshops will take place on December 1-2 and December 7-8, 2013, at the same venue as the main ICCV conference.

Appropriate subjects for workshops include topics from computer vision as well as related fields such as machine learning, computer graphics and human-computer interaction. We encourage submission of workshop proposals that are concerned with ideas/techniques/problems that are relatively new in computer vision.

Proposals that emphasize formats and/or programs that are unique to workshops, such as a large proportion of invited speakers or being based on a competition, are specifically encouraged. Such proposals will be favoured over those that offer a traditional conference-type format.

The proposal should include the following information:

  1. Title, topic and sample call for papers;
  2. Justification of the workshop, including the relationship to the previous ones, the novelty of the proposed workshop, and the reason why the proposed workshop may interest the community.
  3. Names and contacts of the organizers;
  4. Tentative program committee, invited speakers if any;
  5. Estimated attendance;
  6. Estimated number of submissions and acceptance rate;
  7. Estimated breakdown among oral, poster presentations, and invited talks;
  8. Proposed length of the workshop, and date restrictions
  9. Special space and equipment request if any.

 Workshop proposals should be sent to the Workshops Chair, Pushmeet Kohli (pkohli [at] Microsoft [dot] com) with the subject “ICCV 2013 Workshops”.

The deadline for all workshop proposal submission is May 3, 2013. Decisions will be made by end of May 2013. For any additional information, please contact the Workshop chairs:
